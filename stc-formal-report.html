STC Formal Report

I wrote this formal report for a professional writing course (Introduction to Professional Writing) I completed in 2009 at DePaul University. The object of the assignment was to choose an organization to which I belong and to propose a recommendation for some sort of improvement or change within that organization.

As a member of the Society for Technical Communication (STC), this report discusses my recommendation for the creation of an STC student chapter at DePaul University in Chicago. I discuss the benefits that the chapter can provide to both students and the STC as a whole, and basic steps for implementing the chapter. With my recommendation, I also include the primary and secondary research I collected to help explain the benefits of creating a DePaul University student chapter.
