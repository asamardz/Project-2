About Me

I am a first-year graduate student in the Master of Science in Technical Communication and Information Design program at the Illinois Institute (IIT) of Technology in Chicago, Illinois. At IIT, I am also a graduate assistant currently tutoring fellow undergraduate and graduate students in the school's Writing Center. In June 2010, I graduated from DePaul University with a Bachelor of Arts in English with minors in Professional Writing and Interactive Media.

I've always considered writing to be a very important skill and also my strongest strength as a student. My interest in technology has motivated me to learn how writing and technology can be combined into a single discipline to not only provide myself with finalcial stability, but also motivate me to continue writing as technology changes.

Since June 2009, I've been employed as a technical writing intern at Thomson Reuters Markets in Chicago's Loop. I primarily edit, update, and reformat old documentation for financial software applications. While I think the finaicial software industry is interesting, I plan on pursuing a career in another industry like gaming, electronics, or education. I'm very interested in incorporating more interactive applications, like video, into technical communication. I'm also interested in instructional design and how education will evolve as technology continues to change.
