TomTom ONE XL Product Description

I wrote this product description for a technical writing course I completed in 2009 at DePaul University. The object of the assignment was to simply describe a device, rather than to instruct others how to use it. I chose to write about the TomTom ONE XL, a protable GPS navigator, that I have used for the past few years. The primary audience for the document is senior citizens who have trouble looking at maps while driving due to the small print. The completed assignment is a booklet that describes the TomTom ONE XL and its features.

Given the primary audience's age demographic and the reading environment, I chose to write the document in the form of a booklet with large print, short sentences, graphics, and call out boxes.
